What lacks East Boston education among ELL?

Today, East Boston is an extremely diverse neighborhood with the highest percentage of foreign-born of any Boston neighborhood. From 1633, the first European settler started to live on this island. More than XXXXXX people immigrated to East Boston from over XXX countries at the end of 2017. Most of them are English Language Learners(ELL). This project aims at visualize the education situation in East Boston among different ethnicities, by investigate the graduation rate in East Boston High School. Afterwards, these diagrams may tell the story behind or give the researchers a clear direction of the next step to learn more about the education situation in East Boston.

By Chenrui Ye for the course, Information Design & Visualization Studio 2.
